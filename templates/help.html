Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a new scan?
Go to the Scanner tab, enter the URL you want to test, and click “Start Scanning.”
How do I save my scan results?
After your scan finishes, choose “Save” on the results page to persist it in your account.
Can I download my results?
No—on My Saved Scans, only allows Users to search, filter, and delete their scans; downloading will be added soon!
Who do I contact for further help?
Email our support team at support@penshield.com or open a ticket via the Hub.

Contact Us

If your question isn’t covered above, drop us a line at support@penshield.com and we’ll get back to you within 24 hours.
