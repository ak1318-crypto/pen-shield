Cookie Policy

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

2. How we use cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in across different pages.
  • Store your theme and layout settings.
  • Analyze site usage so we can improve our service.

3. Third-party cookies

Our analytics provider may set cookies to gather anonymous usage data. We do not use this data to identify individuals.

4. Managing your cookies

You can always disable or delete cookies via your browser settings. Note that some features (like staying logged in) may not work if you turn cookies off.

5. Contact

Questions about this policy? Please email support@penshield.com.
